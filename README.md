Creates an anki deck from a pdf consisting of every base form of the words from the pdf, their meanings, and the sentence they're found in.

Steps: \
1 - Put input pdf into input/ folder and corresponding Kaikki (kaikki.org) dictionary into dictionaries folder/ \
2 - Click the "Create .csv" button \
3 - Go study!!! \
\
If it throws an error just close and open it again. \
Quality of deck depends on quality of pdf and scan.
